## üìã Features Overview

### 1. Overview Tab (Default)
**What you see:**
- Your current plan details (Premium/Free/Professional)
- Billing amount and next renewal date
- Current payment method
- Recent invoices (last 3)
- Quick action buttons

**What you can do:**
- Click "Change Plan" ‚Üí Jump to Plans tab
- Click "Update Payment" ‚Üí Jump to Payment tab
- Click "Cancel Plan" ‚Üí Open cancellation modal
- View invoice details

---

### 2. Change Plan Tab
**What you see:**
- Three plan cards: Free, Premium, Professional
- Feature comparison
- Current plan highlighted with badge
- Pricing for each tier

**What you can do:**
1. Click any plan card to select it
2. Click "Select Plan" button to confirm
3. Wait for confirmation toast
4. Automatically returns to Overview tab

**Plans:**
- **Free:** $0/month - Basic features
- **Premium:** $49.99/month - Full features (MOST POPULAR)
- **Professional:** $99.99/month - Advanced features

---

### 3. Payment Tab
**What you see:**
- Current payment method (masked card number)
- Expiry date
- Active status badge
- Security information

**What you can do:**
1. Click "Update Payment Method"
2. Enter new card details in modal
3. Submit to update
4. Receive confirmation toast

---

### 4. Invoices Tab
**What you see:**
- Complete invoice history
- Invoice ID, date, amount
- Payment status (Paid/Pending/Failed)
- Download buttons

**What you can do:**
- Click download icon to get invoice PDF
- View all transaction history
- Check payment status

---

## üéØ Common Tasks

### How to Upgrade Your Plan
1. Open Settings page
2. Click "Manage Subscription"
3. Navigate to "Change Plan" tab
4. Click on Premium or Professional card
5. Click "Select Plan" button
6. Wait for confirmation toast
7. Done! ‚úÖ

### How to Update Payment Method
1. Open subscription modal
2. Go to "Payment" tab
3. Click "Update Payment Method"
4. Enter new card details:
   - Card number
   - Expiry date (MM/YY)
   - CVV
5. Click "Update Payment"
6. Wait for confirmation ‚úÖ

### How to Cancel Subscription
1. Open subscription modal
2. Stay on "Overview" tab
3. Scroll to bottom
4. Click "Cancel Plan" (red button)
5. Confirm in modal
6. You'll keep access until next billing date

### How to Download Invoices
1. Open subscription modal
2. Go to "Invoices" tab
3. Find the invoice you need
4. Click the download icon (üì•)
5. Invoice PDF downloads automatically

## ‚ùì FAQs

### Q: When does my plan change take effect?
**A:** Immediately for upgrades. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

### Q: Can I get a refund if I cancel?
**A:** You'll have access until your next billing date. No partial refunds.

### Q: What happens to my data if I downgrade to Free?
**A:** Your data is saved, but you'll lose access to Premium features.

### Q: Can I switch from monthly to yearly billing?
**A:** Not yet implemented. Coming in Phase 2.

### Q: How do I contact support?
**A:** Click "Contact Support" button at the bottom of any tab in the modal.
